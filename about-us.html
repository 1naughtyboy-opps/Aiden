BACKGROUND OF THE ORGANIZATION

A BRIEF LOOK AT THE BACKGROUND OF AIDEN, THE PRESIDENT, AND SOME EXECUTIVES

Yinkfu Bruno Yinyu is the Founder and President of the African Innovation Development and Empowerment Network abbriviated as AIDEN. He has 5years of combined working experience,working and volunteering in other NGO's involved in humanitarian action and environmental conservation activities where he has served in the capacity of a project officer; monitoring and reporting on project grogess, he has also served as a project writer and in other administrative positions. He is a project manager by profession and is currently furthering studies in project Management building a carreer in humanitarian and developmental action in Africa.

The African Innovation Development and Empowerment Network (AIDEN) is an operational non-governmental organoisation created with the vision for development and advancement in Africa our mission is to promote innovative educational, humanitarian, and empowerment activities that are geared towards the advancement and development of Africa by 2063. We match like chariots taking over every corner of our communities bringing empowerment by fostering creativity and innovation in Africa

Innovation Goals

THE INNOVATIVE AFRICA 2063.

The Innovative Africa is the dream of an African continent living its full potentials and making maximum use of its resources. For ages now, Africa has doubtlessly been and is the most endowed continent in-terms of resources both mineral resources and a vast talented and active youth population. When we talk about the African Dream, we refer to the desired moments when Africa will be fully emancipated psychologically and economically. The dream is that moment when we as a continent will be able to transform our resources for our empowerment and development. The African dream is the desire to bring Africa to the place of full development to meet up with other developed counties while maintaining and conserving its rich and unique cultural heritage. 2063 is a strategic year which is inline with African Union Development goals. Therefore as an organization, we create the platform for identifying and promoting innovation in every aspect of development, and we also aim to achieve this dream by incorporating new modern systems of production into our traditional African Systems. Also, we engage communities into long term sustainable development actions.
